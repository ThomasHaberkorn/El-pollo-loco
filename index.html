El pollo loco

How to play:

Licenses:

Impressum:

Use A, Arrow left or to move left. Use D, Arrow right or to move right.
Use Space or to jump. Use w, Arrow up or to throw a bottle.

Kill chicken by jumping on them or throwing a bottle at them.
Collect bottles to throw them at the Endboss.
Kill the Boss by throwing multiple bottles at it.
